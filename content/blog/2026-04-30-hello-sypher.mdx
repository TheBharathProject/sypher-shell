This is the first post. Mostly to prove the blog works. A little to put down what Sypher is.

## The shape of the bet

I'm building a studio. Not one product. Not one big platform. A small set of small tools, each one focused on a single specific job, each one priced for the rupee, each one shipped before it's perfect.

The bet is that:

- ten focused tools beat one cluttered platform
- a tool that does one job well will find its audience faster than a tool that tries to do five
- if you keep the unit cheap and the surface small, you can afford to ship a lot of them

That's it. No bigger thesis. The work is the proof.

## What this blog is for

Three things:

1. **Notes from the workshop** — what's being built, what shipped, what didn't work
2. **Decisions out loud** — the trade-offs behind a price, a stack choice, a feature cut
3. **Posts I'd want to read** — the kind of writing I look for when I'm trying to figure something out

It's not a launch channel. Launches happen on Product Hunt and X. This is the slower thing — the part where you write down what you learned so the next person (often future-you) doesn't have to learn it again.
